# VOICE: Atrium

> How it sounds. The gene the Build and Taste loops read for copy.

Status: ASSEMBLED from canon (VISION.md, README.md, shipped UI copy), 2026-06-10.

## Register / tone

Plain declarative. Calm, present, unhurried; the register of a good facilitator
who speaks rarely and is right when they do. Short sentences. States facts
without hedging. Warm but never chummy.

## Languages

English leads. Spanish is first-class, not a translation afterthought (the
language toggle sits in the pre-join flow, es-US). Meeting rooms in this
portfolio are bilingual; copy must survive both.

## Lexicon (exact words)

- "a third participant" (the product, in one phrase)
- "the board" (never "the dashboard", never "the feed")
- foreground / midground / background (the zones; lowercase in prose)
- "the board has a tide" (decay, in one image)
- "earns": the board fills as the conversation earns it; capabilities are earned, not shipped
- "salience" (what the engine weighs)
- "leave-behind" (what survives a meeting)
- "Atrium is on the line" (the consent line, said out loud)
- "Hey Claude" (the wake phrase; Atrium answers to Claude in the room)

## Do / don't

- Do write UI copy as quiet fact: "The board is empty. It fills as the conversation earns it."
- Do attribute plainly: "Jose will send the SOW by Friday." Owner first, no flourish.
- Do say what Atrium does not do; the refusals are part of the product.
- Don't hedge ("possibly", "seems like") in anything Atrium itself displays.
- Don't anthropomorphize past participant ("Atrium hears" is fine; "Atrium feels" is never).

## Anti-voice (never say)

- No em dashes or en dashes anywhere a human reads. Use commas, periods, parentheses.
- Never "AI-powered", "revolutionary", "supercharge", "10x", or any growth-hype register.
- Never surveillance vocabulary as product verbs: monitor, track, surveil
  (sessions are "saved", meetings are "archived"; the distinction is doctrinal).
- Never "users" for the people in a meeting; they are participants.
- Never promise memory or capabilities the Trust Ladder has not yet granted.
