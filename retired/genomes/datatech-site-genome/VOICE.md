# VOICE: Data Tech

> How it speaks. The gene the copy loop reads so language is on-brand by construction.

Status: MINED 2026-06-12 from the corporate deck + Jose's draft. Sources in EVIDENCE/.

## Register

Confident B2B wholesale distributor: restrained, technical, premium. One statement, one action,
lots of air. The voice is the antidote to the busy storefront, the same move the design makes.
Three decades of trust, said plainly, never hyped.

## Language

- **Primary: Spanish.** DataTech serves Centro America, Sur America, Caribe Espanol (PR, RD),
  and Caribe Ingles. Spanish-primary, English secondary. Copy decisions default to ES.
- Tone in Spanish stays corporate-confident, not casual.

## Lexicon (from the corporate deck, usable as site copy)

- "Tres decadas de confianza e innovacion"
- Growth pillars: **Crecer / Solucionar / Gestionar / Abastecer**
- "Data Tech Inc., founded 1996, Miami FL"
- New demand areas (deck language): retail exhibition solutions, data-center infrastructure,
  energy / technical conditioning.

## Anti-voice (reject on sight)

- Marketing superlatives and hype (best, premier, stunning, must-see).
- Clutter copy and tile-grid density. Air is the brand.
- Em dashes, en dashes, emojis.
- Fabricated company facts. Brand-share and growth numbers come from the deck, cite the source.
