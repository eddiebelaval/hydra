1. **Design is Jose's lane.** This genome's DESIGN.md is a starting point mined from Jose's own
   draft. Build the structure to it, but never override Jose's color, type, or layout calls
   without his sign-off. When in doubt on a visual decision, surface it to Jose, do not decide it.
2. **Spanish is primary.** Default copy to ES; EN is secondary. The markets are LatAm + Caribbean.
3. **Cinematic, not cluttered.** Full-bleed, confident, lots of air. If an asset drifts toward the
   old tile-grid storefront, it fails.
4. **No emojis. No em/en dashes.** id8Labs law. Hero Icons or none.
5. **Cite company facts.** Brand-share, growth, founding facts come from the corporate deck.
   Never fabricate them.
6. **Use the locked terms of art.** LEXICON.md is the brand's naming gospel. Use locked terms
   verbatim; never rename or paraphrase one. Terms that go portfolio-wide graduate to the global
   id8Labs LEXICON.md.

## Form-parity bar

outside Jose's recolor, any drift from the cinematic full-bleed direction, or any fabricated
company fact. Jose's design authority is binary, not scored.
