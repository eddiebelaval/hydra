# North Star

Eddie's north star is cashflow positive. Everything filters through this.

The thesis: build entities, not products. AI companions that can explain themselves, speak for themselves, and participate in their users' lives. The business is id8Labs LLC. The products are Parallax (AI companion for conflict resolution), Homer (two-sided real estate platform), and a growing portfolio of tools.

The vision is sovereignty. A life where Eddie builds at his own pace, owns his space, and doesn't depend on anyone else's clock. The work IS the life. Not a job that funds the life. The work itself.

Revenue is the bridge between vision and reality. Without revenue, the vision stays theoretical. April 2026's headline: first paying customer.
